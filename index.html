Work Experience


Programming Intern at Gamelogic Pte Ltd

As part of self improvement, during a free trimseter where I had no lessons, I decided that I would challenge myself to find a company where I could intern at to improve myself and broaden my knowledge on the development cycle of games. During my time at Gamelogic, I got to learna plethora of stuff that was simply not taught in schoool especially not in the first year.

During my time at Gamelogic, I was working on a game called Fishdom, an arcade fishing game where I helped implement UI and gameplay features according to the Designer's requirements as well as bug fixes spotted by the QA team.
Throughout my time, I began to pickup on a multitude of skills that ranged from a scripting language i was new to which was Lua, and furthermore I was also introduced to many Industry Standard tools, like Gitlab and the Atlasssian Softwares like Jira and Confluence. Daily scrums were common as well as Sprint Meetings every week which allowed me to furhter learn to workflow and how new features are implemented and managed.

Software Developer Intern at Mitsuboshi Overseas Headquarters

As part of my attachment to Mistuboshi Overseas Headquarters pte ltd, Me and 3 other interns worked as a closely knitted group to not only learn the processes that the workers go through but to also help figure out ways to digitalize the material issuing process.
Throughout the internship, not only did get hands on experience with how the factory workers do their daily task, we also got to experience the full work flow from receiveing materials and products, to issuing out materials and products for production and shipment respectively. As such, there were multiple projects developed during the period.

One such project, which was also the main one that we were tasked to do was the Warehouse Material Issuing System, a system that was developed to ensure that materials were correctly issued based on the quanitity and lot number requested. This was due to an incident that happened earlier where because of a small mix up between 2 similar ingredients, the company had suffered a significant loss and thus as interns it was our job to help them find out a way to prevent these incidents from recurring.
This project had also been nominated as a finalist in the 2019 SICC Award for the Most Scalable Collaboration

The projects were developed in C#, using SQLite for Data Storage as well as Excel for other functions like printing QR Codes and forms
